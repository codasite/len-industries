LEN INDUSTRIES is a Kentucky-based asphalt maintenance and striping company.

Founder Patrick Wilson, graduated from Boyle County High School in 2004. He worked in construction from the year 2000 up until he enlisted to serve our great nation in the United States Marine Corp in 2009. After honorably serving, he returned to construction from 2013-2022. In 2022 he chose to start his own company. LEN Industries is named after his 3 sons, Luke, Evan, and Noah. Patrick's dream is to one day hand LEN Industries down to them.
